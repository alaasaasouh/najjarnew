About Us

Najjar Trading was founded in 1976 by Dr. Abdallah Najjar, initially specializing in glass products. Over the years, the company has expanded its operations to include aluminum, metal, and composite panel works, thereby broadening its expertise to meet the evolving demands of the market.

Thanks to the diversity of its product range, Najjar Trading possesses the unique capability to undertake comprehensive and integrated projects, delivering complete solutions across multiple disciplines. The company is firmly committed to providing high-quality products that adhere to the highest industry standards.

This commitment is upheld through continuous staff training, the use of state-of-the-art equipment and tools, the implementation of rigorous quality control procedures, and close collaboration with trusted partners and suppliers.
