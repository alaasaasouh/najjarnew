About Us

Najjar Trading was established in 1976 by Dr. Abdallah Najjar as a company specializing in glass. Over time, we have evolved to include aluminum, metal, and composite panels, expanding our business to meet diverse market needs.

Due to the variety of our products, Najjar Trading has the advantage of contracting complete and coherent solutions for complementary multi-tasking projects. Our policy is to provide quality products that meet the highest standards.

This is meticulously and continuously monitored through regular team training, state-of-the-art equipment and tools, adopted quality control procedures, and collaboration with our partners and suppliers.
